Mobster Bar Brawl has been cancelled. This project started development when Max Townsend was in college and continued for a few years after the fact, but there was never enough content put together to fully justify a release, and the direction of the project needed serious work and an overall better plan.

It was a sprite based first person shooter with a modern spin on the classic fps genre. It featured a goofy vector based art style, tight shooting mechanics, and an absurd over the top setting.

 We may return to this concept one day but it is not currently in devleopment.

You can stay up to date with Max's latest projects on his twitter.
